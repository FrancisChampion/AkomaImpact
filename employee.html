About Akoma Impact Academy

Akoma Impact Academy is a youth empowerment initiative under Mmabunu Global, designed to equip SHS graduates with practical skills, mentorship, and entrepreneurial tools to thrive in today’s economy. Through hands-on vocational training, leadership development, and community engagement, Akoma bridges the gap between school and meaningful employment—empowering young people to become creators, leaders, and changemakers in Ghana and beyond.

Whether you're looking to learn a trade, launch a venture, or gain access to mentorship and guidance, Akoma is where your journey begins.
